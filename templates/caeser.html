Welcome to the Caeser Cipher Challange!


The Caeser Cipher was invented by Julius Caeser who used it to send mesage to his commanders by shifting the characters in the message 3 letters along in the alphabet.

Eg. A would become D and B would become E etc.


To decode the shift the letters are moved back by three letters with D going back to A.
